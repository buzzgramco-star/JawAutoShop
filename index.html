About Us

Craftsmanship
You Can Trust

Jaw Auto Service has been Ayr's trusted destination for premium automotive care. Nestled in the heart of Ayr, Ontario, our shop combines old-world attention to detail with modern diagnostic technology — delivering service that meets the highest standards every single time.

We believe every vehicle deserves the same level of care and precision — from daily drivers to weekend classics. Our licensed technicians treat your car as if it were their own, offering honest assessments, transparent pricing, and workmanship you can count on.
