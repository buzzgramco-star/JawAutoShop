About Jaw Auto Service

Our Story

Built on Trust,
Driven by Excellence

Jaw Auto Service was founded with a single mission: to provide the Ayr community with automotive care that is truly trustworthy. Located at 148 Northumberland St Unit 6, Ayr, ON, our shop has grown into one of the area's most respected service centres — built not on flashy promises, but on consistently outstanding results.

As a locally-owned and operated business, we understand what matters most to our customers: honest assessments, fair pricing, and skilled workmanship that stands the test of time. Every vehicle that comes through our doors receives the same level of care and attention — whether it's a routine oil change or a complex diagnostic job.

Our team of licensed technicians brings a wealth of experience across all makes and models. We stay current with the latest automotive technology to ensure your vehicle receives modern, accurate service — every time.
